What is Arts Stream?

When a student chooses arts as a stream, it is thought that the options available are lower than those who take up science or commerce. Breaking this myth, arts career options have increased over time, and hence today, many individuals are making their names in prominent arts stream jobs.
Many subjects one studies during their journey in arts include history, psychology, economics, political science, geography, philosophy, Hindi, and others. Studying these varied subjects helps explore arts stream jobs like law, journalism, hotel management, literature, and fashion designing, to name a few.
